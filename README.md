#  SUPER STORE SALES DASHBOARD
## Project Objective
   Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard using a cleaned and structured sales dataset from Excel pivot analysis to visualize business performance and regional insights.
Integrated Excel-based data modeling with Power BI to deliver a dynamic, data-driven view of customer and sales operations.
## Data Set Used
- <a href="https://github.com/grangasairishik-rgb/Data-Analysis-Dashbord/blob/main/INTcustomerdata1.xlsx"> Dataset </a>
## Questions (KPIS)
Total Sales – Overall revenue generated from all customer transactions.
Total Quantity Sold – Aggregate count of products sold across all orders.
Total Profit – Net profit derived after deducting costs from total sales.
Sales by Month and Year – Trend analysis of sales performance over time.
Profit by Month and Year – Monthly profit progression for performance tracking.
Sales by Region – Comparative view of sales distribution across Central, East, South, and West regions.
Sales by Category & Sub-Category – Product-level profitability and contribution insights.
Sales by Segment – Analysis of customer segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
Sales by Payment Mode – Share of revenue from Online, COD, and Card transactions.
Sales by Ship Mode – Evaluation of delivery modes (Standard, Second, First Class, Same Day) and their sales impact.
Return Rate – Percentage of returned orders relative to total sales volume.
Shipping Delay (Days) – Difference between order and ship dates to assess operational efficiency.
